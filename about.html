Who We Are

About EcoMap

A digital sustainability initiative aimed at powering a cleaner, greener Greater Western Sydney.

Our Story

Our community first came together with a simple goal: to make recycling information easy, local, and actionable for everyone in Western Sydney.

With limited accessible resources showing where you actually recycle different materials (from bottles and metal to electronics and green waste), we built this platform to bridge that gap — helping people find recycling centres, understand what they accept, and make more sustainable choices.

🌏

Western Sydney Focus

Built by locals, for locals.

The Big Picture

Large Scale Impact

Western Sydney processes thousands of tonnes of recyclable materials annually, from community centres to industrial facilities.

Local Operations

Local councils and recycling partners operate dozens of facilities across the region — each helping divert waste from landfill into reuse or recycling streams.

Community Action

Public participation rates in recycling efforts continue to rise thanks to better access to information and accurate facilities listings.

Our Mission

To empower Western Sydney residents with clear, accurate, and up-to-date information on local recycling options — so that everyone can make informed choices and take real steps toward a circular, sustainable future.

Our Vision

A community where every resident knows where to recycle what, understands the impact of their actions, and feels confident contributing to a zero-waste, environmentally responsible Western Sydney.
